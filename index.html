About Us

Gardens Produce INC. is a family owned business established in December 2020 in the produce industry. Our mission at Gardens Produce is to provide quality produce and build long lasting relationships with our valued customers. We understand how important quality and outstanding customer service is. We deliver to small business, restaurants and catering companies. Our goal is to provide our customers with top quality product which is guarenteed to last longer, allowing them to save time, money and therefore reduce food waste.
